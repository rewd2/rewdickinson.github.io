*See the Important Credit Card Terms and Conditions for TD Easy Rewards, TD Cash Rewards, TD First Class, TD Aeroplan and TD Ameritrade Client Rewards for details about APRs, fees, eligible purchases, balance transfers and program details. For TD Easy Rewards and TD Cash Rewards, eligible purchases do not include purchases of any cash equivalents, money orders, and/or gift cards or reloading of gift cards.

For TD First Class credit cards, bonus miles will be reflected on your credit card statement 6 to 8 weeks after a qualified first purchase and/or 6 to 8 weeks after $3,000 in total net purchases made within the first 6 billing cycles of your credit card account opening date. This offer is non-transferable.

For TD Easy Rewards credit cards, maximum bonus accumulation for this promotional offer is 20,000 Easy Rewards bonus points, which can then be redeemed for $200 cash back.

For the TD Cash Rewards credit cards, maximum bonus accumulation for this promotional offer is a $200 cash rewards, which can be redeemed as a statement credit only.

Provided your TD Easy Rewards or TD Cash Rewards Credit Card account is open and in good standing, the 20,000 Easy Rewards bonus points or $200 Cash Rewards statement credit will be reflected in the Points Program or Cash Rewards Summary of your Credit Card statement in 6 to 8 weeks after you spend $1500 in total net purchases within 90 days of account opening. This offer only applies to new TD Easy Rewards or TD Cash Rewards Credit Card accounts during the promotional period and is non-transferable.

For TD Aeroplan credit cards, a one-time welcome bonus of 25,000 Aeroplan Miles will be added to your Aeroplan member account if at least $1,000 in total net purchases are made within the first 3 billing cycles of the account opening date. Net purchases means new purchases minus credits, returns, and adjustments.
